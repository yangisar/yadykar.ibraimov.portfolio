Photography

Magnum Photos — Beyond the Silence Collaboration

"Tono-naan is at home,
but where is home?"

A photoproject exploring the Uyghur genocide. Yadykar turns his lens on Kazakhstan's eastern border and Uyghur territory, documenting the experiences of survivors of Chinese concentration camps.

"Families torn apart, cultures wiped out, and lives changed forever. This is not some distant tragedy — it is happening right now to people with names, families, and dreams, many of whom are still suffering in silence."

The project explores how the borderland has become more than a conflict zone — a place of torture on one side and a sought-after refuge on the other; a political border that divides the landscape and threatens the fate of its people.
