Yadykar
Ibraimov

Almaty, Kazakhstan
@yangisar ↗

Uyghur–Kazakh artist working at the intersection of documentary film, photography, and literature. His work traces the human cost of the Uyghur genocide — following survivors, borderlands, and silences across Central Asia and beyond. Recognized by Magnum Photos, screened at international festivals from Cottbus to Angkor, and published in the first Uyghur literary anthology in English.
